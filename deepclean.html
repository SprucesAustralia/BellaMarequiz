Deep Cleaning Guide

Everything you need to know about deep cleans at Bella Mare.

What is a Deep Clean?

A deep clean is a thorough, top-to-bottom clean of an entire apartment or villa. Unlike a regular departure clean (which takes ~1.5–2 hours), a deep clean takes approximately 7–8.5 hours and covers every surface, corner, and detail in the property.

Deep cleans are scheduled periodically – typically when there is a gap between guest bookings. The hotel management decides which rooms need deep cleaning and when.

Key Facts

👥 2 People Required

Deep cleans must always be done in pairs. You will be paired with another cleaner.

⏰ 7–8.5 Hours

Each room takes a full working day. Plan for a long shift.

💰 Fixed Rate Pay

You are paid a fixed total rate per room, split between the 2 cleaners.

📅 Scheduled by Management

You cannot self-schedule deep cleans. Management will assign them.

Deep Cleaning Rates

Fixed rates – split between 2 cleaners.

Room Type Duration Total Rate Per Person (÷2)
2 Bed Apartment ~7 hours $224 $112

Rates are fixed regardless of day of week. No bonus system applies to deep cleans – these are flat-rate jobs.

What Does a Deep Clean Involve?

A deep clean goes far beyond a standard departure clean. Here is what is expected:

Kitchen

  • • Inside and outside of all cupboards
  • • Inside oven, microwave, rangehood
  • • Inside fridge and freezer
  • • Behind and under appliances
  • • All splashbacks and surfaces
  • • Dishwasher interior clean

Bathrooms

  • • Scrub all grout and tile surfaces
  • • Remove all mould from shower areas
  • • Behind and around toilets
  • • Inside vanity cupboards
  • • Clean exhaust fans
  • • Polish all fixtures and fittings

Living Areas & Bedrooms

  • • Remove cushions and vacuum under/behind furniture
  • • Tops of skirting boards and door frames
  • • Top of AC units
  • • Inside all wardrobes and drawers
  • • All corners and cobwebs
  • • Windows inside and tracks

General

  • • All light switches and power points
  • • TV unit, table under TV
  • • Balcony/patio clean
  • • Laundry area including washing machine
  • • Entry area and front door
  • • Report any maintenance issues

How Deep Clean Scheduling Works

1

Hotel identifies rooms for deep cleaning

Based on gaps in bookings and room condition. The hotel decides which rooms and when.

2

Management pairs cleaners and confirms dates

You will be paired with another cleaner. Deep cleans always require 2 people working together.

3

You are notified via WhatsApp / app

Check your messages and the app for your deep clean assignments. Do not assume – always confirm.

4

Work together for the full day (~7–8.5 hours)

Plan for a full day. Bring water, snacks, and wear comfortable clothes. Start early when possible.

5

Hotel inspects the room

If issues are found, you may need to come back and address them. The standard must be very high – every surface, every corner.

Important Notes

• You cannot schedule your own deep cleans. They are assigned by management based on hotel availability.

• Deep clean weeks can be busy – typically 30–40 hours per week when active across all rooms.

• Quality must be exceptional. The hotel will inspect thoroughly. If areas are missed, you will need to return to fix them.

• Regular departure cleans may still happen on the same days. Check your schedule carefully.

• Deep cleans are a great opportunity to earn more. When they start, make sure your availability is updated in the app.
