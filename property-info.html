Welcome to Bella Mare Beachside Apartments!

Italian for “Beautiful Sea,” Bella Mare Holiday Apartments are an attractive 3.5-star Mediterranean-style complex conveniently located just across the road from picturesque Greenmount and Coolangatta Beach and a short stroll to the restaurants, clubs, and shops of Coolangatta and Tweed Heads.

Popular Rainbow Bay beach is also within easy walking distance, and the fun and excitement of Twin Towns Services Club is just across the road from our Coolangatta Accommodation. Buses to Surfers Paradise and the major tourist attractions depart from right outside our door, and the onsite tour desk can organise all your tours for you.

Key Housekeeping Information

Building & Access

  • Floors: Up to four levels, no elevators, only stair access.
  • Parking: Free underground (limited) and nearby street parking.
  • Fire Safety: Smoke detectors in each unit, stairwells for evacuation. Never prop open fire doors or block exits.

Cleaning Responsibilities

  • Departure Clean: After each guest checkout.
  • Weekly Clean: For stays longer than 10 days.
  • No Daily Housekeeping: Unless specially requested.
  • Focus Areas: Bathrooms, kitchens, bedrooms, balconies/patios; proper trash removal and recycling; reporting damages.

Room Types & Features

Room Type Typical Occupancy Key Features
2-Bedroom (Single Level) Up to 4 guests Two bedrooms, often 2 bathrooms
2-Bedroom (Villa) Up to 4 guests Internal stairs; split level; same amenities
3-Bedroom Villa Up to 6 guests Largest size, multiple levels, 2+ bathrooms

All have kitchens, laundry, and possibly a balcony or patio.

Cleaning Standards

  • High-Profile Areas: Mirrors, taps, toilets, kitchen counters, floors.
  • Appliances: Oven, microwave, fridge, washer/dryer—clean inside/out.
  • Bedding: Change all linens on departure cleans; weekly linen changes for long-stay guests.
  • Balconies/Patios: Sweep and wipe outdoor furniture.
  • Chemicals: Use properly; store securely (never in guest rooms).

Additional Tips

  • Keep stairwells clear; do not block with carts.
  • Secure doors after cleaning; respect guest privacy.
  • Report broken items, leaks, or suspicious activity immediately.
  • Adhere to fire safety: if alarm sounds, evacuate via stairs immediately.
  • Wear PPE when handling chemicals or sharp objects.
  • Never keep lost-and-found items; inform management if you find valuables.

Thank you for maintaining high standards and safety at Bella Mare. Cleanliness and professionalism help ensure positive guest experiences and reviews!
