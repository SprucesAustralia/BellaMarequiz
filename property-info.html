Welcome to Bella Mare Beachside Apartments!

Italian for “Beautiful Sea,” Bella Mare Holiday Apartments are an attractive 3.5-star Mediterranean-style complex conveniently located just across the road from picturesque Greenmount and Coolangatta Beach and a short stroll to the restaurants, clubs, and shops of Coolangatta and Tweed Heads.

Popular Rainbow Bay beach is also within easy walking distance, and the fun and excitement of Twin Towns Services Club is just across the road from our Coolangatta Accommodation. Buses to Surfers Paradise and the major tourist attractions depart from right outside our door, and the onsite tour desk can organise all your tours for you.

Property & Layout

  • The resort/property has around 25–26 apartments in total.
  • There are 8 stairwells, each containing around 7 apartments (a mix of 2-bedroom apartments and 2-bedroom villas at the top).
  • No elevators; all floors are accessed by stairs.
  • There is one gym on-site that needs daily cleaning (vacuuming + equipment wipe-down).
  • There is 1 three-bedroom apartment in the complex; the rest are primarily 2-bedroom apartments (or villas).
  • Each apartment has its own laundry facilities (washer and dryer).
  • Most apartments have a shower over the bath in the second bathroom; only about 2 apartments have shower curtains (the rest have glass panels).
  • No dedicated spa baths; there are no large spa tubs requiring special cleaning.
  • The complex has no lifts, so cleaners typically bring supplies to the bottom of each stairwell and then carry items up.
  • Thursday is generally the quietest day.
  • Vacuuming of stairwells is required once a week; this can be spread out, as long as each stairwell is done weekly.
  • The gym requires daily vacuuming and wiping down of equipment.
  • Management is open to flexible scheduling. If it gets busier, more cleaners can be called in via a WhatsApp broadcast system.
  • Check-ins are generally from 2 PM; check-outs at 10 AM. Some guests leave earlier, and about two requests per week come in for late check-outs.

Cleaning Scope & Standards

  • Departure cleans: Full clean, including kitchen, bathrooms, making the beds with fresh linen, etc.
  • Mid-stay service (for guests staying 10+ nights):
    • Change bed sheets and towels.
    • Basic clean of bathrooms (wipe surfaces, etc.).
    • Tidy kitchen and restock amenities (dishwasher powder, tea towels, etc.).
    • No vacuum/mop (apartments each have a vacuum for guest use).
  • Bedrooms: Typically, a two-bedroom apartment has one queen bed and two singles. The three-bedroom has two queen beds plus two singles.
  • Bedding style: Most apartments use two sheets and a blanket or throw at the foot of the bed. In winter, doonas are provided and laundered/stored accordingly.
  • Rollaway beds & port-a-cots: Housekeepers/managers usually provide a separate linen pack for the guests to set up themselves.
  • Dishwashers: Each apartment has one; guests are asked to run it prior to checkout, so dishes are generally not an issue.
  • Lost property: On-site managers do a quick sweep after guests depart; found items are stored in the office until claimed.
  • Stair cleaning gear: There is a stair climber trolley with triangular wheels available to reduce physical strain when carrying linens/supplies upstairs.

Communication & Allocation

  • Management currently uses WhatsApp to communicate with housekeeping staff.
  • A 48-hour notice is typically given for the next day’s cleans; updates are often sent again the afternoon prior.
  • Larger scheduling overviews can be provided up to a week in advance but can change based on bookings or cancellations.
  • Management (Neil and one additional manager) are on-site and can assist with tasks like room stripping if it’s very busy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Are there elevators in the complex?

A1: No, all floors are accessed by stairs. The property does not have any lifts.

Q2: Do any apartments have spa baths?

A2: No, there are no dedicated spa baths or large tubs that require special cleaning.

Q3: What are the check-in and check-out times?

A3: Standard check-in is 2 PM, and check-out is by 10 AM. Some guests may leave earlier or request a late check-out.

Q4: Are rollaway beds made by housekeeping?

A4: Typically, no. Housekeeping provides a linen pack for rollaway beds and port-a-cots, which guests set up themselves.

Q5: Is there a gym on-site and how is it maintained?

A5: Yes, there is one gym on-site. It is vacuumed and the equipment is wiped down daily.
