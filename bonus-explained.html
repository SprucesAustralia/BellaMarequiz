Why the $10 Bonus Exists

The full explanation – so it makes sense why a perfect room is worth $10 more.

The $10 is a BONUS you EARN – not a penalty.

Your base rate is guaranteed. The $10 is added on top when the room passes inspection with no issues.

So why does the $10 even exist?

Good question. The $10 isn’t random – it reflects the real cost to the business every time a room is not up to standard. Even a tiny fix-up triggers a chain of unpaid admin work that Bella Mare has to absorb.

Paying cleaners an extra $10 for rooms done right the first time is cheaper than paying for the chain reaction that follows a fix-up. That’s why the bonus exists – and why it disappears when the chain is triggered.

Important: The hotel hired us to clean to their standard. In a normal day, their staff should not need to check our work – the expectation is every room is guest-ready the moment we finish.

Hotel staff only step in when something was missed. So when they do check, that itself is a signal that the standard wasn’t met. The bonus isn’t lost because they “inspected” – it’s lost because they had to step in at all.

The Hidden Cost of a Fix-Up

Every single fix-up sets off this chain – all of it unpaid work for the business.

1.Hotel staff has to stop and inspect the room. They shouldn’t need to – but they do.
2.They find an issue and take photos as evidence.
3.Photos get sent to management.
4.Management reviews the photos and logs the issue.
5.Management contacts the cleaner to explain what was found.
6.Cleaner (or someone else) goes back to fix it.
7.Management confirms it’s resolved with the hotel.
8.Trust with the hotel takes a small hit – which affects the whole team.

“But I went back and fixed it in 2 minutes.” Even so – steps 1 to 5 and step 7 still happened. That’s the cost the $10 bonus is designed to prevent.

The Simple Business Math

✓ Room is perfect

  • Cleaner earns +$10 bonus
  • Zero admin work
  • Hotel stays happy
  • Everybody wins

✕ Fix-up needed

  • Cleaner still gets base rate
  • No $10 bonus earned
  • Admin chain kicks in
  • Trust with hotel dips

In one sentence:

Perfect room → you earn +$10 → no one has to chase anything. That’s the deal.
