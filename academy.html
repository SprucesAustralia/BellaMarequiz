Welcome to the Bella Mare cleaning reference page. Here you'll find all the essential cleaning lists, notes, and reminders to ensure our high standards are maintained.

CLEANING CHECKLIST (Things to check)

General Cleaning Tasks

  • Clean all surfaces, including side tables
  • Vacuum floors
  • Make beds properly
  • Check that all lights are functioning properly (especially bathroom lights)
  • Ensure sofas/cushions are completely dry after professional cleaning
  • Check that sliding doors to balconies open and close properly

Bedding and Linens

  • Ensure blankets stay on the beds unless instructed otherwise
  • Place towels in all bathrooms
  • Distribute bath towels appropriately across bathrooms, not all in one
  • Follow specific linen instructions for certain rooms

Kitchen and Appliances

  • Check and empty dishwashers if necessary
  • Stock milk in the fridge for specific rooms
  • Check that fridges and freezers are clean
  • Leave food in the fridge when specified
  • Ensure each kitchen box has 2 dishwasher sachets
  • Report full or broken dishwashers immediately
  • Ensure dishwashers are emptied when needed

Trash and Recycling

  • Place bin liners in all rubbish bins
  • Do not put bin liners in recycling bins

Restocking Supplies

  • Check and restock cleaning supplies
  • Ensure availability of toilet rolls

Windows and Fixtures

  • Clean windows when specified
  • Check that all lights are functioning
  • Report any maintenance issues with doors or windows

Room Specific Instructions

  • Follow any unique instructions for specific rooms
  • Follow specific instructions for rooms with owners' belongings
  • Leave food in the fridge when specifically instructed
  • Report any locked appliances (e.g., fridges) immediately

Keys and Access

  • Return keys to the black key drop box at the end of the day
  • Front gate lockbox code will be provided#
  • Linen room lockbox code will be provided

NOTES, TIPS AND REMINDERS LIST

General Procedures

  • Clarify arrival/parking with management; front gate lockbox code will be provided#
  • Secure keys daily and always return to the office/dropbox; linen room lockbox will be provided
  • Report room start/finish times and any issues immediately via group chat or app
  • Always clean Back-to-Back (B2B) rooms first as new guests are arriving that day
  • Use a fresh mop head per room, no liners in recycling bins, and lock all doors upon exit
  • Manage linen bags correctly and dispose of all apartment rubbish in the main garbage area, not by the dryer
  • Immediately report and photograph exceptionally dirty rooms or any damages found
  • Constantly check the group chat/app for frequent schedule changes and updates from management
  • Wear a black t-shirt if possible; shorts are acceptable attire
  • "Service" cleans are for occupied rooms (10 am-3 pm); announce entry and focus on linen, towels, and a light clean
  • Use the cleaning app/tracker for all room status updates
  • Return keys to the black key drop box at the end of the day
  • Be aware of deep clean scheduling and requirements

Communication and Reporting

  • Report full/broken dishwashers; ensure they run and have two cleaning sachets
  • Report all maintenance issues found (e.g., leaks, broken items) with photos if possible
  • Immediately report any guest items found (lost and found) to management
  • Handle service clean entries politely if guests are present but unaware
  • Report when starting and finishing each room via the app
  • Notify management immediately if a room is exceptionally dirty with photos
  • Report any maintenance issues found (e.g., lights not working, doors not opening properly)
  • Check for special instructions regarding owners' belongings or food in fridges

Cleaning Standards and Supplies

  • Stock bathrooms with one full toilet roll and one spare only; check cupboards
  • Ensure correct towels, scourers, and cloths are present and tidy utensil drawers
  • Thoroughly clean floors (especially sandy ones), showers, all surfaces, ovens, and balconies
  • Use a maximum of 10ml of Bobby Dazzler in mop handles; rinse handles daily
  • Return dirty mop heads as instructed and don't leave supplies by the dryer
  • Allow sofa cushions to fully dry after professional cleaning
  • Use a fresh mop head per room and wash them after use
  • Verify all lights are functioning in each room
  • Check that sliding doors to balconies open and close properly

Inventory and Room Setup

  • Verify crockery/cutlery has sets of 6; report missing or remove excess to the office
  • Ensure furniture (e.g., coffee tables) is correctly positioned per room standards
  • Be prepared to assist colleagues with rooms if requested by management
  • Pay special attention to rooms after professional carpet/upholstery cleaning
  • Check dishwashers are emptied and running properly

COMMON ISSUES OF FIX UPS LIST

PAYMENT BONUS INFORMATION

A $10 bonus is added to your base rate when no fix ups are required. The 'No Fix Ups' rate includes this $10 bonus when quality standards are met upon first inspection. The 'Fix Ups Required' rate applies if any basic housekeeping issues listed below need correction.

Bathroom Issues

  1. No bathmat in ensuite
  2. Shower is mouldy and smells
  3. Toilet needs a wipe/scrub
  4. Floor needs a mop in bathroom
  5. Incorrect bath mat used instead of hand towel
  6. Shower has sand in the bottom
  7. Missing hand towel in bathroom
  8. Bathroom surfaces need scrubbing with green brush
  9. Streaky mirrors or glass in bathroom
  10. Shower screen scum not removed
  11. Vanity not properly wiped

Kitchen and Appliance Issues

  1. Kitchen drawers need tidying
  2. Oven needs tin foil on racks
  3. Sink needs drying off
  4. Recycling bin needs wiping out
  5. Fridge needs a wipe inside and out
  6. Missing green scourer in dish wash caddy
  7. Missing dish wash powder/sachets
  8. Missing cloth in kitchen caddy
  9. Kitchen surfaces not wiped (benchtop crumbs)
  10. Sink unclean

Floor and Surface Issues

  1. Floor hasn't been mopped properly in living area
  2. Floors are sandy, need vacuuming and mopping again
  3. Sand on dining table and side tables
  4. Missed dusting on reachable surfaces (tables, sills)
  5. Floors not properly vacuumed (visible debris)
  6. Floors not properly mopped (streaks)

Waste Management Issues

  1. Rubbish bin lid needs a wipe over
  2. Bin needs a liner
  3. Recycling bin not emptied
  4. Rubbish bins not emptied

Item Placement Issues

  1. Patio/balcony needs a tidy/clean
  2. Items not replaced correctly (towels, amenities)
REMINDER: A $10 bonus is added to your base rate when no fix-up issues are found upon inspection. If any of the issues listed above are found, the room will need to be fixed and the bonus will not be applied to that room.
