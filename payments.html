Payments & Finance

Below are the standard pay rates and average cleaning times (departure clean):

Room Type Pay Rate Average Time
2 Bed $60 ~1 hour 30 minutes
2 Bed Split $70 ~1 hour 45 minutes
3 Bed $85 ~2 hours

Note: Actual cleaning time may vary depending on:

  • Size and layout of the unit
  • Number of occupants
  • Condition of the apartment upon arrival

Weekly Service

A weekly service takes place if a guest has stayed for over 10 days. The rate for a weekly service is 40% of the standard departure clean:

Room Type Weekly Service Rate Average Time
2 Bed $24 ~1 hour 30 minutes
2 Bed Split $28 ~1 hour 45 minutes
3 Bed $34 ~2 hours

Additional Work

From time to time, we may request extra work. This will be paid in blocks of 15 minutes, each block paying $10 AUD.

Payment Method

Please note, we pay via ABN (Australian Business Number), not TFN (Tax File Number), as this work falls under legislation for contracted services.
