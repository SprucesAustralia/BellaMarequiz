Payments & Finance

Below are the standard pay rates and average cleaning times (departure clean):

Room Type: 2 Bed

Pay Rate: $60

Average Time: ~1 hour 30 minutes

Room Type: 2 Bed Split

Pay Rate: $70

Average Time: ~1 hour 45 minutes

Room Type: 3 Bed

Pay Rate: $85

Average Time: ~2 hours

Note: Actual cleaning time may vary depending on:

  • Size and layout of the unit
  • Number of occupants
  • Condition of the apartment upon arrival

Weekly Service

A weekly service takes place if a guest has stayed for over 10 days. The rate for a weekly service is 40% of the standard departure clean. The time for a weekly service is also 40% of the departure cleaning time:

Room Type: 2 Bed

Weekly Service Rate: $24

Average Time: ~36 minutes

Room Type: 2 Bed Split

Weekly Service Rate: $28

Average Time: ~42 minutes

Room Type: 3 Bed

Weekly Service Rate: $34

Average Time: ~48 minutes

Additional Work

From time to time, we may request extra work. This will be paid in blocks of 15 minutes, each block paying $10 AUD.

Payment Method

Please note, we pay via ABN (Australian Business Number), not TFN (Tax File Number), as this work falls under legislation for contracted services.
