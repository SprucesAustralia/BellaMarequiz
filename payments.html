Training Pay Structure

For the first two rooms cleaned together:

  • Trainer: Receives full standard room rate.
  • Trainee: Receives 1/3 of the standard room rate.

Team Splits

Total room rate is divided evenly by cleaners:

  • 2 Cleaners: 50% of rate each (Time is halved).
  • 3 Cleaners: 33% of rate each (Time is 1/3).

Quality Standards & The $10 Bonus

Basic Issues (Bonus Lost)

  • ● Missed dusting on reachable surfaces
  • ● Floors not vacuumed/mopped properly
  • ● Streaky mirrors or glass
  • ● Bathroom details (toilet, shower scum)
  • ● Kitchen crumbs or unclean sink
  • ● Rubbish bins not emptied

Acceptable Issues (Bonus Kept)

  • ● Stubborn wall marks or scuffs
  • ● Carpet stain removal
  • ● Deep oven/rangehood cleaning
  • ● Extensive mould removal
  • ● Maintenance issues (drains, bulbs)
  • ● Excessively dirty (beyond normal use)
